Built for Studying Optimization

Tick-King is an online learning solution fueled by spaced-repetition algorithms in an effort to optimize the study process. the preview page. Users have the option to study decks with our learning system or review decks on their own free time.

Concept

Tick-King was built for those looking for a learning solution that helps users master concepts -- not just study aimlessly. By using our guided learning tools, users will have decks given to them in randomly timed intervals until they have mastered the concept.

Networking

Connect with peers to aid you in your studying journey!

At Your Own Pace

If you want to study at your own pace, you also have the option to review study decks at your convenience.
